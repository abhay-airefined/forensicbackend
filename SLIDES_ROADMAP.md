## SLIDE 4: Use Case — Scholarly Publishing

### Protecting Academic Research

**Problem:** AI models trained on millions of journal articles without consent

**Solution:**
- Bulk attribution scanning of article databases
- Publisher-level dashboards
- Automated infringement alerts
- Evidence packages for licensing negotiations

**Scale:** 5M+ articles, 16K+ publications potential

---

## SLIDE 5: Use Case — Trade Publishing

### Author & Rights Protection

**Problem:** Bestselling books appearing in AI training sets

**Solution:**
- Per-title forensic analysis
- Rights & royalties impact reporting
- Author notification system
- Evidence for litigation support

**Scale:** Global book market, 4M+ new titles/year

---

## SLIDE 6: Use Case — Digital Library Platforms

### Embedded Attribution Layer

**Problem:** Libraries need to protect hosted content

**Solution:**
- Real-time attribution widget (embeddable)
- Institution-level reporting
- API integration with library systems
- Proactive monitoring service

**Scale:** 25,000+ institutions worldwide

## SLIDE 7: Use Case — Legal & Consulting

**Solution:**

- Multi-jurisdiction support
- Consulting partnership model

**Market:** $15B+ IP litigation market
