# First AIDA — Executive One-Pager

## AI Forensic Attribution for the Publishing Industry

---

### The Problem

Generative AI models are trained on copyrighted content without publisher consent, threatening intellectual property rights, author royalties, and the fundamental economics of publishing.

**Publishers need:** Scientific, court-admissible evidence to prove their content was used in AI training.

---

### The Solution: First AIDA

**First AIDA** is an enterprise AI forensic platform that determines with scientific certainty whether AI models were trained on specific copyrighted content.

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **30+ Forensic Agents** | Independent detection methods running in parallel |
| **Bayesian Evidence Fusion** | Court-grade statistical aggregation |
| **Legal-Ready Reports** | Multi-language memoranda for litigation |
| **Publishing Integration** | Native connectivity with Ingenta ecosystem |
